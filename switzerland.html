Key insight

Wealth drives visits

There is a clear positive correlation between GDP per capita and visitor numbers — wealthier countries tend to send more tourists to Switzerland.

Top senders

Germany, France, the UK, and Italy dominate, each contributing over 200k–1M visitors annually, clustering in the upper right of the chart.

Outliers

Norway, Ireland, and Luxembourg have very high GDPs but send relatively few visitors, likely due to small populations or geographic distance. Iceland shows unusually low and volatile visitor counts.

COVID impact

A sharp dip is visible across most trendlines in 2020–2021, followed by a strong recovery through 2022–2025.
