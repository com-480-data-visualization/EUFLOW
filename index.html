Problem statement

Tourism is one of the most important economic and cultural activities in Europe, but travel patterns are often difficult to understand through raw statistics alone. Our project aims to present fragmented tourism data in a clear, intuitive, and engaging interactive way.

Objectives

EUFlow enables users to discover where Europeans travel, when they travel through seasonal patterns, how much they spend, and why Switzerland receives visitors by examining geography and economic factors such as GDP.

Target audience

Designed for travelers looking for inspiration, students exploring real-world data, and policymakers or tourism organizations seeking to better understand visitor behavior and regional tourism demand.

Why this topic

As international students living in Switzerland, we became curious about how Switzerland fits into broader European tourism patterns — and how geography, seasonality, and economic conditions influence travel decisions.
